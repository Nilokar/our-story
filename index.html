Where It All Began

Barcelona

A surprisingly effective scheme.

We met at work in Barcelona and spent three years as colleagues.

There had always been something between us, but somehow life had not quite found the right moment.

Eventually, Fredrik came up with an elaborate scheme to ask Nilo out.

It involved two parrots...
...and cooking the Easter Bunny.

Knowing that Nilo loved to paint, he asked if she would create something for his apartment. In return, he promised to cook the Easter Bunny.

Nilo knew exactly what he was doing.

He knew that she knew.

She decided to play along.

It was a surprisingly effective scheme.

It gave us two parrots.
Rabbit stew.
And our first date.

The painting still hangs in our home today.

The Pins

The Bucket List

A map, a little competition, and a lot of adventures.

One Christmas, Fredrik gave Nilo a world map and a box of coloured pins.

It started as a way to keep track of where we had been. It quickly became a bucket list, along with a healthy amount of competition.

We each had our own colour, with another for the places we had visited together.

Shared trips changed the pins. Sometimes solo trips did too.

More than twenty-five countries later, we've discovered that the more places we visit, the longer our list becomes.

Some trips were for the memories. Others were clearly about the pins.

Relationship Test Björnrike Sweden

The Relationship Test

Björnrike

Stronger than the IKEA test.

Some people say assembling IKEA furniture is the ultimate relationship test.

Given that Fredrik is Swedish, that probably would have been the sensible option.

Instead, he decided to teach Nilo how to snowboard.

It was Nilo's first visit to Fredrik's family cabin in Björnrike and her very first time on a snowboard.

Fredrik approached his new role as snowboard instructor with great confidence.

The mountain did not share his optimism.

What followed was a day of spectacular falls, questionable coaching, and a growing suspicion that this might not have been Fredrik's best idea.

More than five years later, the relationship is still going strong.

Nilo still has not been back on a snowboard.

Home Is More Than One Place

Where We Come From

Before we built one together.

Before building a home together, we first discovered the places that had always been part of each other's world.

Västervik

Fredrik took Nilo to the summer house where he had spent countless childhood summers.

Suddenly, the Swedish archipelago made a lot more sense.

It was not just a beautiful place. It explained a little more about Fredrik.

Bergen

Bergen is where Nilo grew up, the city that shaped her and where many of the people she loves most still are.

Bergen also happens to be Europe's rainiest city.

Against all reasonable expectations... it did not rain.

Between the mountains, the sea and unexpectedly blue skies, Fredrik got to know another part of Nilo's world.

The Road to Vienna

A Home of Our Own

One car, one long drive, one new life.

Eventually, it was time for a new adventure.

We packed up our lives in Barcelona.

A moving truck.
Three pets in the car.
Countless boxes.
One destination.
Vienna.

Somewhere between Barcelona and Vienna, home stopped being a place.

It became whatever we were building together.

Not everyone enjoyed the move.

South Africa

Look at the Seals.

A surprisingly effective distraction.

On our last morning in South Africa, we got up early and hiked to the top of the Cape of Good Hope.

It was just the two of us.
Perfect weather.
A breathtaking view.

It felt like the perfect ending to an unforgettable trip.

Then Fredrik pointed into the distance.

"Look at the seals."

It was a surprisingly effective distraction.

When Nilo turned around, Fredrik was already on one knee.

Yes.
